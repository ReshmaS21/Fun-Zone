Memory games can actually go a long way in helping out with your cognitive functions. Memory games have been proven to help out in several ways, such as your brain’s ability to concentrate as well as critically think about how to solve problems.

Sudoku

Sudoku is one of the world’s most popular puzzle games, but it is often misunderstood. Many people think that Sudoku is a game of luck – they think that you should have to guess which numbers belong in each empty place.

Snake and Ladder

Snakes and ladders is a board game for two or more players regarded today as a worldwide classic. The game originated in ancient India as Moksha Patam, and was brought to the UK in the 1890s. It is played on a game board with numbered, gridded squares.
